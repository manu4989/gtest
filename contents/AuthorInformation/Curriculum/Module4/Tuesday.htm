The Tuesday (Module 4) Topic

Click here to view the live topic described by this topic.

This topic outlines what the typical Tuesday of the fourth week will hold. The features employed in this topic are Snippets, Dynamic HTML (DHTML) Drop-downs and playing "local" videos. (a local video is where a video clip exists among the other files in the system).

Look at the snippets, Dynamic HTML, and Multimedia sections in the RoboHelp Reimagined project to learn more about the features used.
