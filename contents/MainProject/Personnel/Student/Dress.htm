Dress

While Learn A Latte is a school it is also a working coffee shop. Dress is casual, but we do prefer that you look presentable and somewhat professional.

Things that are frowned upon:

  • Denim pants that have been cut off to make shorts
  • Extra short shorts
  • Swimwear
  • Flip Flops

*NOTE: The list above is subject to change at any given time. Please check this topic often to see if there is anything new.
